FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build my website?
Most projects complete in 5–14 days depending on complexity. Simple WordPress sites take 5–7 days, Shopify stores take 7–10 days, and custom apps take 2–4 weeks.
Do you provide support after launch?
Yes! I offer 2 weeks of free support after every project for bug fixes and minor changes. After that, affordable monthly maintenance packages are available.
Can I update the website myself after it's done?
Absolutely. I'll train you and hand over full access. WordPress and Shopify are especially easy to manage yourself — no coding needed.
Do you work with international clients?
Yes! I work with clients in Pakistan, UAE, UK, USA and beyond. All communication is online via WhatsApp, email, or video call.
What payment methods do you accept?
Bank transfer, Easypaisa, JazzCash, PayPal, and Wise. I take 50% upfront and 50% on delivery — so you only pay in full when you're happy.
What if I'm not happy with the result?
I offer unlimited revisions until you're 100% satisfied. Your happiness is the goal — I won't close a project until you love it.
